# Chapter 14: Safety and Ethics in Robotics

This chapter addresses the critical issues of safety and ethics in robotics, including safety standards, ethical frameworks, and responsible development practices for AI-integrated robotic systems.

## Learning Objectives

- Understand safety standards and risk assessment in robotics
- Learn about ethical frameworks for AI and robotics
- Explore safety mechanisms and fail-safe systems
- Consider the societal impact of humanoid robots

## Technical Explanation

Safety and ethics in robotics encompass the technical, legal, and moral considerations that must be addressed when developing and deploying robotic systems. This includes functional safety, risk assessment, ethical decision-making, and responsible AI practices.

## Summary

Safety and ethics are fundamental considerations in robotics that must be integrated from the design phase through deployment and operation to ensure responsible and beneficial use of robotic technologies.
